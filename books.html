Recommended Books

A curated selection of books I recommend to clients working through addiction recovery, betrayal trauma, relationship healing, and sexual health. These resources offer valuable insights and practical tools for your journey.

Out of the Shadows

Patrick Carnes

A foundational guide to understanding and recovering from sex addiction. Carnes provides a comprehensive framework for understanding the addiction cycle and pathways to recovery.

Sex Addiction

The Betrayal Bond

Patrick Carnes

Essential reading for partners who have experienced betrayal trauma. Explores the complex psychological bonds that form in traumatic relationships and how to break free.

Betrayal Trauma

Mating in Captivity

Esther Perel

A groundbreaking exploration of desire in long-term relationships. Perel challenges conventional wisdom about intimacy and helps couples navigate the paradox of love and desire.

Sex Therapy

The State of Affairs

Esther Perel

A nuanced look at infidelity that goes beyond judgment to explore what affairs reveal about relationships and how couples can heal and grow after betrayal.

Infidelity Recovery

Come as You Are

Emily Nagoski

A science-based guide to understanding female sexuality and desire. Empowers women to understand their bodies and create fulfilling sexual experiences.

Sex Therapy

Hold Me Tight

Sue Johnson

Based on Emotionally Focused Therapy, this book helps couples understand their attachment needs and create deeper emotional bonds. Essential for rebuilding trust and connection.

Couples Therapy

The Body Keeps the Score

Bessel van der Kolk

A comprehensive guide to understanding trauma and its effects on the body and mind. Essential for anyone healing from betrayal trauma or other traumatic experiences.

Trauma Recovery

Wired for Intimacy

William Struthers

Explores how pornography affects the brain and relationships from a neuroscience perspective. Helpful for understanding the impact of compulsive sexual behaviors.

Sex Addiction

The Seven Principles for Making Marriage Work

John Gottman

Based on decades of research, Gottman provides practical strategies for building stronger relationships. Valuable for couples at any stage of their relationship journey.

Couples Therapy

Healing the Shame That Binds You

John Bradshaw

A powerful exploration of toxic shame and its role in addiction and relationship dysfunction. Provides tools for healing and reclaiming your authentic self.

Recovery

The Porn Trap

Wendy & Larry Maltz

Addresses the impact of pornography on individuals and relationships. Offers practical guidance for both those struggling with compulsive use and their partners.

Sex Addiction

Getting the Love You Want

Harville Hendrix

A guide to Imago Relationship Therapy that helps couples understand their relationship patterns and transform conflict into connection. Excellent for couples rebuilding after betrayal.

Couples Therapy
